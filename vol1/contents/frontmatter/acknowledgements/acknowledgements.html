Acknowledgments

This book is inspired by the TinyML edX course and CS294r at Harvard University. It also traces back to an early moonshot for TinyML with Pete Warden: running keyword spotting on a microcontroller. It represents years of collaboration with students, researchers, and practitioners who have shaped its development. We are deeply indebted to the folks whose groundbreaking work laid its foundation.

Through this collaboration, our understanding of machine learning systems deepened, and we realized that fundamental principles apply across scales, from tiny embedded systems to large-scale deployments. This realization shaped the book’s expansion beyond TinyML to provide foundations applicable across all scales of machine learning systems implementation.
