## üîí **How Protection Works**

**Namespace Strategy**: 
All our custom extensions live in `mlsysbook/` directory.

When someone runs `quarto add ute/custom-numbered-blocks`, it will:
- ‚úÖ **NOT** affect our `mlsysbook/custom-numbered-blocks` directory
- ‚úÖ Create a new `ute/custom-numbered-blocks` directory 
- ‚úÖ Leave our customizations completely intact

**No conflicts possible** - the `mlsysbook/` namespace is exclusive to this project!
