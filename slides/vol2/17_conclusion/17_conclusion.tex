\begin{frame}{Principle Tensions: The Art of Trade-offs}
\note{
% -- LINK: What prior concept connects to this slide
The six principles must all be satisfied. But they sometimes CONFLICT ---
this slide reveals the tensions engineers must navigate.

% -- NARRATE: What to SAY while showing this slide
[3 min] Walk through the diagram: ``Communication optimization increases
synchronization, which increases failure exposure. Sustainability limits
raw infrastructure choices. Responsible AI adds 10--20 ms latency to serving.''
Key insight: ``The art of distributed ML engineering is finding designs that
balance all six within acceptable trade-offs.''

% -- WARN: What students will get wrong on THIS topic
Common error: students optimize one principle and ignore the others. Correct
framing: production systems must satisfy all six simultaneously --- a system
that is fast but unfair, or robust but unsustainable, fails to deploy.

% -- FLEX: [CORE] + contingency
