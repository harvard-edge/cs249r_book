\multicolumn{6}{@{}l}{\cellcolor{lightgray!50}\textbf{Represent}} \\
\texttt{Tn} & Tensor & The fundamental mathematical structure holding information (scalars, vectors, matrices). & \texttt{Pr} & Probability & The mathematical primitive for representing uncertainty — distributions, densities. \\
\texttt{Pm} & Parameter & The irreducible learned memory or state of an algorithm (weights, biases). & \texttt{Eb} & Embedding & The fundamental algorithmic act of mapping a discrete symbol into continuous space. \\
\texttt{Sp} & Sample & The irreducible unit of empirical data distribution (a single data point). & \texttt{Tp} & Topology & The fundamental structural assumption placed on data (Sequence, Grid, Graph). \\
\texttt{Hs} & Hidden State & The architectural primitive for persistent intermediate representation. & \texttt{Fc} & Factorization & Approximating a massive matrix as the product of smaller ones (Low-Rank). \\
\texttt{Os} & Optim State & The irreducible memory of the optimization process (momentum, velocity). & \texttt{Cc} & Caching & Holding intermediate state in fast memory to prevent recomputation (e.g., KV Cache). \\
\texttt{Cp} & Checkpointing & Saving and restoring model state for fault tolerance or memory efficiency. & \texttt{Ir} & Int. Rep. & The software state of a computation graph before hardware execution (ONNX, PT2). \\
\texttt{Sr} & SRAM & On-chip, low-capacity, extremely high-bandwidth memory (Registers, Scratchpads). & \texttt{Dr} & DRAM & Off-chip, high-capacity, lower-bandwidth memory (HBM, DDR). \\
\texttt{As} & Artifact Store & Durable, distributed storage for trained models and datasets (S3, Model Registry). & \texttt{Rc} & Record & The fundamental atomic unit of raw information (a single row, image, or document). \\
\texttt{Ds} & Dataset & A structured collection of records. & \texttt{Ix} & Indexing & The high-dimensional partitioning of vector space (e.g., HNSW) for sub-linear retrieval. \\
\texttt{Vr} & Virtualization & The abstraction of physical memory via page tables (e.g., PagedAttention) to solve fragmentation. & \texttt{St} & State & The mathematical representation of an environment or context (RL, SSMs). \\
\multicolumn{6}{@{}l}{\cellcolor{lightgray!50}\textbf{Compute}} \\
\texttt{Op} & Operator & The mathematical action of mapping one space to another (linear or non-linear transforms). & \texttt{Dd} & Dense Dot & The irreducible algorithm for fully connected, all-to-all information transformation. \\
\texttt{Cv} & Convolution & The irreducible algorithm for local, weight-shared spatial information transformation. & \texttt{Po} & Pooling & The algorithmic primitive for spatial or temporal reduction (Max, Average). \\
\texttt{Sm} & Sampling & The primitive for stochastic selection from a probability distribution. & \texttt{At} & Attention & Letting data dynamically decide which other data it interacts with. \\
\texttt{Gt} & Gating & Using data to scale or shut off other data (Multiplicative flow). & \texttt{Nm} & Normalization & The transform that re-centers and re-scales data distributions between layers. \\
\texttt{Ro} & Routing & Conditional data direction to specific sub-units (e.g., Experts). & \texttt{Qz} & Quantization & Reducing the bit-width of numbers (FP8, INT4). \\
\texttt{Sp} & Sparsification & Turning dense compute sparse by forcing weights or activations to zero. & \texttt{Fs} & Fusion & Merging multiple operations into a single execution kernel to minimize memory IO. \\
\texttt{Bt} & Batching & Grouping independent inputs for parallel processing. & \texttt{Ti} & Tiling & Partitioning computation into sub-blocks to optimize for memory hierarchy. \\
\texttt{Cl} & Compilation & Lowering high-level operators into hardware-executable kernels. & \texttt{Ma} & MAC Unit & Multiply-Accumulate unit. The fundamental silicon logic gate for tensor math. \\
\texttt{Vu} & Vector Unit & Single Instruction, Multiple Data (SIMD) ALU. The silicon primitive for parallel arithmetic. & \texttt{Ex} & Exec Engine & The production worker node that executes compiled graphs on incoming requests. \\
\texttt{Tr} & Transform & The deterministic action of altering raw data (cropping, resizing, parsing). & \texttt{Ag} & Aggregate & Combining multiple records into summary statistics. \\
\texttt{An} & Analog ALU & Continuous-voltage compute unit (e.g., memristor, optical) for extremely low-power inference. & \texttt{Ac} & Activation & Non-linear functions (ReLU, GELU) providing expressive power. \\
\texttt{Wa} & Weight Avg & Averaging model weights across time or distributed workers (e.g., SWA, EMA). & & & \\
\multicolumn{6}{@{}l}{\cellcolor{lightgray!50}\textbf{Communicate}} \\
\texttt{Cr} & Chain Rule & The fundamental mathematical mechanism that allows composed derivatives to be computed. & \texttt{Ad} & Autodiff & The algorithmic primitive that mechanically computes exact derivatives through arbitrary control flow. \\
\texttt{Tk} & Tokenization & Segmenting raw input into discrete processing units. & \texttt{Sk} & Skip/Res & The fundamental primitive of identity mapping. Allows information to bypass computation. \\
\texttt{Fb} & Feedback & The structural primitive of routing a signal backward in the graph (Recurrence). & \texttt{Ws} & Weight Sharing & The structural optimization of communicating the same learned state across multiple functional paths (e.g., CNNs). \\
\texttt{En} & Ensembling & Merging weights or outputs across time/workers to improve generalization (SWA). & \texttt{Pl} & Pipelining & Overlapping the execution of sequential stages across different compute units. \\
\texttt{Sy} & Sync / Coll & Aggregating and broadcasting state across distributed devices. & \texttt{Pf} & Prefetching & Proactively moving data into faster memory tiers before it is needed. \\
\texttt{Ic} & Interconnect & The physical wiring moving data between silicon components (NoC, PCIe, NVLink). & \texttt{Rt} & HW Router & Silicon logic that directs packets across the physical interconnect. \\
\texttt{Rp} & RPC Protocol & The synchronous network protocol for moving data between distributed services. & \texttt{Mq} & Msg Queue & The asynchronous network primitive for buffering and streaming data (Kafka). \\
\texttt{Fl} & Flow/Stream & The continuous movement of raw data from source to system (ETL, Kafka). & \texttt{Fm} & Format & The structural encoding of data for storage or transit (Parquet, TFRecord). \\
\texttt{Re} & Retrieve & Fetching stored state or external knowledge (e.g., from a KV Cache or Vector DB). & & & \\
\multicolumn{6}{@{}l}{\cellcolor{lightgray!50}\textbf{Control}} \\
\texttt{Ob} & Objective & The mathematical formulation of the goal (Argmin/Argmax). & \texttt{Cs} & Constraint & The mathematical primitive for defining bounds and restrictions on variables. \\
\texttt{Gd} & Grad Descent & The core control loop: takes communicated gradients and updates Parameters. & \texttt{Rw} & Reward & A scalar control signal evaluating the quality of an action (RL). \\
\texttt{Iz} & Initialization & The algorithmic control for setting the starting state of parameters. & \texttt{Mk} & Masking & The structural enforcement of causality or prevention of information leakage. \\
\texttt{Sc} & Scheduling & Dynamically decaying or modulating control signals over time. & \texttt{Rg} & Regularization & The structural penalty applied to the objective to force simpler solutions. \\
\texttt{Tm} & Termination & The control primitive that evaluates conditions to halt an iterative optimization loop. & \texttt{Al} & Allocation & The dynamic assignment of hardware resources to software tasks. \\
\texttt{Ar} & Arbiter & Hardware logic that schedules instructions and manages contention. & \texttt{Ck} & Clock/Sync & The hardware primitive for temporal control, synchronization, and barriers. \\
\texttt{Ld} & Load Balancer & The fleet-level control unit routing incoming requests to available hardware. & \texttt{Oc} & Orchestrator & The fleet-level control plane that scales, restarts, and manages the lifecycle of execution nodes (e.g., K8s). \\
\texttt{Fi} & Filter & The deterministic logic that includes or excludes records based on predicates. & \texttt{Sm} & Schema & The structural constraint defining the expected types and fields of a record. \\
\texttt{Ro} & Routing & The dynamic, data-dependent dispatch of tensors (e.g., Mixture of Experts). & \texttt{Rs} & Resilience & The systemic countermeasures (checkpointing, elastic recovery) for macroscopic hardware decay. \\
\texttt{Ct} & Critic & The value function evaluating the expected return of a state (Actor-Critic RL). & & & \\
\multicolumn{6}{@{}l}{\cellcolor{lightgray!50}\textbf{Measure}} \\
\texttt{Dv} & Divergence & The mathematical quantification of distance between distributions or tensors (e.g., KL, L2). & \texttt{Lf} & Loss Function & The specific algorithmic computation of the mathematical distance (e.g., Cross-Entropy). \\
\texttt{Rf} & Receptive Fld & The measurement of how far information can travel within the architecture in one pass. & \texttt{Id} & Info Density & The measure of optimization efficiency (Bits per Parameter). \\
\texttt{Ut} & Utilization & The percentage of theoretical hardware capacity actively used (MFU). & \texttt{Ew} & Energy & The physical power consumed to perform computation (Joules/token). \\
\texttt{La} & Latency & The end-to-end time from user request to final response. & \texttt{Av} & Availability & Service Level Agreement metric measuring uptime and fault tolerance. \\
\texttt{Vl} & Volume & The physical size or cardinality of the dataset (Bytes, Row Count). & \texttt{En} & Entropy & The Shannon information-theoretic limit; the absolute bound on data compressibility. \\
\texttt{Td} & Thermodynamics & The ultimate physical limitation (Landauer limit, thermal throttling) capping system scale. & & & \\
